you have an atproto handle. you like music. let's go.

plyr.fm is an audio streaming platform built on [atproto](https://atproto.com), the open protocol behind [Bluesky](https://bsky.app) and [BlackSky](https://blacksky.community). sign in with your atproto handle (sometimes called your [internet handle](https://internethandle.org)) and start listening.

## your first 5 minutes

1. **sign in** — go to [plyr.fm](https://plyr.fm) and enter your atproto handle (e.g. `you.bsky.social`)
2. **find a track** — browse the feed or hit `Cmd+K` to search by name or mood
3. **play it** — click any track to start streaming
4. **like it** — hit the heart. your like is stored in your [PDS](https://atproto.com/guides/self-hosting#pds) as an atproto record you own
5. **build a playlist** — click "add to playlist" on any track, name your collection

that's it. your likes, playlists, and listening data live in your personal data repo — not locked in plyr.fm's database.

## what you get

- **stream music** from independent artists who own their data
- **like, comment, and build playlists** — all stored as [atproto records](/lexicons/overview/) in your PDS
- **timed comments** — leave reactions at specific moments in a track
- **mood search** — search by vibe, not just keywords (`Cmd+K` → "rainy afternoon jazz")
- **jams** — create shared listening rooms and listen with friends in real time

## keyboard shortcuts

| key | action |
|-----|--------|
| `space` | play / pause |
| `j` | previous track |
| `l` | next track |
| `q` | toggle queue |
| `cmd+k` | search |
| `m` | mute / unmute |

## leaving

you can leave plyr.fm at any time. your atproto records stay on your PDS by default, but you can choose to delete those too. for the full technical details, see the [offboarding documentation](https://github.com/zzstoatzz/plyr.fm/blob/main/docs-internal/offboarding.md).

## start listening

head to [plyr.fm](https://plyr.fm) and hit play.
